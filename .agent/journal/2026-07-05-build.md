## Concept
Geometry Dash-style auto-runner platformer — new genre #80. Distinct from #48 Neon Sprint (3-lane endless runner). This is a side-scrolling obstacle course with jumping, gravity flips, and procedural level generation.

3. Full `src/index.ts` (~1,448 lines): procedural levels, 5 obstacle types, 4 collectible types, 8 game modes, 3 difficulties, 40 achievements, 8 skins, 5 themes, gravity flip, combo scoring, XP progression, particles, trails

## Issues Encountered
- **TypeScript errors (6 total):** `ConeGeometry` and `PlaneGeometry` not in `@iwsdk/core` type declarations despite being available at runtime via `three`. `@iwsdk/core/dist/runtime/three.d.ts` does `export * from 'three'` but `three` 0.185.1 ships no types and `@types/three` isn't installed. Fixed by replacing `ConeGeometry` with `CylinderGeometry(0, ...)` and `PlaneGeometry` with `BoxGeometry(w, 0.001, d)`.
- Also fixed: `target` → `lookAt` in camera config, `Group` cast for `createTransformEntity`, `addEventListener` cast on `Text`, implicit `any` type annotation.
- **sharp not found:** `@iwsdk/vite-plugin-dev` imports `sharp` but it wasn't in node_modules. Copied from neon-poker.

## Lessons
- `ConeGeometry` and `PlaneGeometry` are NOT in `@iwsdk/core` type declarations. Use `CylinderGeometry(0, radius, height, segments)` for cones and `BoxGeometry(w, 0.001, d)` for flat planes.
- When copying node_modules between projects, verify the target structure matches (no nested directories).
